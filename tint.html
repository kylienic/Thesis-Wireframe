You can select highlights, midtones or shadows for color balance adjustment. Once adjusting color balance, you shift image colors towards desired colors and away from undesiredones.

Tint ranges from green to red, and dictates what shade of undertone the image has. To adjust tint, first calculate the tint level. Tint level is an integer value between -180 and 180 that represents the angle between the current image tint and the desired tint.

Color temperature ranges from blue [k] to gold [k]. Color temperature can be adjusted in post-processing of RAW images. It is most directly impacted by white balance.
